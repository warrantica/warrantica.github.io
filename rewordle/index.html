Rewordle is a blatant rip-off of WORDLE, without the infuriatingly charming once-per-day limit and the social aspects.

Go play the original first!

How to play:

The goal is to guess the correct 5-letter word in 6 tries. Each guess reveals some hints for the answer.

A
Green letter means the letter is in the correct position.

B
Yellow letter means the letter is not in this position, but is somewhere in the word.

C
Grey letter means the letter is not in the word.
